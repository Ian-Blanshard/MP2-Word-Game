Welcome to Word Game

The game where you try to make the longest word you can in 30 seconds, to test your English language skills.

How to play

    click start game
    12 letters will appear on the diplay tiles
    click the letters to make the longest word you can
    your word must be a minimum of 3 letters to count
    you have 30 seconds to complete your word
    when your word is complete click submit word
    See if you can beat your previous score
